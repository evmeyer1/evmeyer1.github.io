One of the first projects I coded was the game 0h h1, this logical game involves having an even number of red tiles and blue tiles in each column and row, while making sure that no rows or columns are identical and avoiding having three of the same color in a row.

The birthday calculator uses algorithms in order to determine on which day of the week you were born, as well as being able to show you the next ten leap years, and being able to tell which day of the week your birthday will be on for the next ten years.
